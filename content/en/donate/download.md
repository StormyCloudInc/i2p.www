### Why Donate to I2P?

I2P is a **free, open-source project** built and maintained by volunteers around the world. We're committed to providing secure, private, and censorship-resistant communication for everyone.

Your donations help us:

- **Develop new features** and improve performance
- **Maintain infrastructure** (download servers, update systems, websites)
- **Support developers** who dedicate their time to privacy technology
- **Keep I2P free** for users everywhere

### How Your Support Makes a Difference

I2P has no corporate backing, venture capital, or advertising. We depend entirely on community support to keep the network running and growing.

Every contribution, no matter the size, helps us continue our mission to protect online privacy and freedom.
